⚔ ShatteredCrown — How to Play

Overview

ShatteredCrown is Battle Chess — pieces have HP and fight on contact. The game ends when a King is captured (checkmate) or no legal moves remain (stalemate).

Combat

  • Click a piece, then click a square to move or attack.
  • If the defender survives, the attacker stays in place and the turn ends.
  • If the defender's HP reaches 0, it is captured and the attacker moves to its square.

King Abilities

Higher Morale

Spawn a friendly Rook on any empty square. King recovers 25% max HP.

Za Wardou (Toki wa Tomare)

Time stops — the opponent loses their next turn.

Dirty Deeds

Swap the King's position with any other friendly piece.

Epitath

Reverse the opponent's last move — the board reverts to before they moved.

Tip: Stunned pieces show a purple ★. King abilities appear in the panel when the King is selected and the ability hasn't been used.
