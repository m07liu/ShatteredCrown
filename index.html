How to Play

Battle Chess

Pieces have HP. Moving onto an enemy attacks it — not an instant capture. Reduce HP to zero to claim the square. Survive a hit and the attacker stays put, turn ends.

💀
Last Stand

About to land a killing blow? A challenge screen appears — type 5 random letters in 7 seconds to execute the capture. Fail, and your turn ends with nothing gained.

✨
Piece Abilities

Every piece type has a unique power. Knights chain up to 3 attacks, Bishops heal allies in a 3×3 area, Rooks pierce the enemy behind their target, Queens stun and tax HP.

👑
King's Choice

Before each match both Kings pick one once-per-game ability: spawn a Rook, stop time, teleport with a friendly piece, or reverse the opponent's last move.

🌋
Battlefield Maps

Pick from 4 maps with live hazards. Volcano tiles deal 10 damage per turn, water blocks movement and line-of-sight, repair squares boost Attack, fog hides enemy pieces.

⚔ ShatteredCrown — How to Play

Overview

ShatteredCrown is Battle Chess — pieces have HP and fight on contact. The game ends when a King is captured (checkmate) or no legal moves remain (stalemate).

Combat

  • Click a piece, then click a square to move or attack.
  • If the defender survives, the attacker stays in place and the turn ends.
  • If the defender's HP reaches 0, it is captured and the attacker moves to its square.

Pieces & Abilities

♙PawnHP 40 · ATK 10
End-of-TurnStrength in Numbers — Each pawn adjacent to a friendly pawn gains +1 HP at end of turn.
♖RookHP 50 · ATK 20
PassiveIts Hammer Time — Every attack pierces through to the enemy directly behind the first target, dealing full damage to both. Shows ⊕ on the pierce.
♘KnightHP 60 · ATK 12
ActiveHippity Hoppity — Attacking opens chain mode (teal rings). Attack up to 3 enemies in sequence at knight-jump distance from the last hit. Knight always returns to origin. Click Skip Chain to end early.
♗BishopHP 30 · ATK 20
ActiveI'm Pope-ing Off — Select the bishop and click ⛪ I'm Pope-ing Off. Heals all friendly pieces in the 3×3 area by 7 HP. Costs the turn.
♕QueenHP 100 · ATK 25
PassiveStunning Beauty — Hit pieces are ★ STUNNED and cannot attack next turn.
PassiveHP Tax — +10% of target's current HP as bonus damage. Shows +N TAX.
♔KingHP 35 · ATK 4
Once/MatchKing's Choice — Choose one of four abilities before the game. Use it once, free action (no turn cost). Shown when King is selected.

King Abilities

Higher Morale

Spawn a friendly Rook on any empty square. King recovers 25% max HP.

Za Wardou (Toki wa Tomare)

Time stops — the opponent loses their next turn.

Dirty Deeds

Swap the King's position with any other friendly piece.

Epitath

Reverse the opponent's last move — the board reverts to before they moved.

Environment Tiles

Maps like Hybrid, Flood, and Foggy scatter special tiles across the board. Switching maps resets the match.

🔥 Volcano

Deals 10 damage to any piece standing on it at end of turn. The lava does not discriminate.

🌊 Water

Impassable. Pieces and sliding line-of-sight (Rook, Bishop, Queen) cannot cross water tiles.

⚙ Repair

Permanently grants +5 Attack to any piece that steps on it. One-time buff per visit.

🌫 Fog

Enemy pieces hidden in fog are invisible unless you have a friendly piece in an adjacent square.

Tip: Stunned pieces show a purple ★. King abilities appear in the panel when the King is selected and the ability hasn't been used.
